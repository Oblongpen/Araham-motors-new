Our Journey

Key milestones in Araham evolution

2019

Company Founded

VoltEdge Motors was established with a vision to revolutionize electric mobility and sustainable transportation.

2020

First Prototype

Unveiled our first electric vehicle prototype, showcasing breakthrough battery technology and innovative design.
